Smart Building Control

Marasi

As part of its vision to lead the smart integration landscape, ECDM executed a cutting-edge project using KNX technology in collaboration with EMAAR. This initiative aimed to create a fully intelligent environment by integrating centralized control systems for lighting, HVAC, curtains, and more ensuring exceptional comfort and operational efficiency across residential and commercial spaces. Executed with top-tier technical precision and rapid delivery, the project highlights ECDM's role as a trusted partner in delivering forward-thinking smart solutions tailored for the future.

Light systems

Suez Canal University

As part of ECDM’s continuous contribution to upgrading public and educational facilities, the company successfully delivered advanced lighting systems for the Family Medicine Center at Suez Canal University. The scope involved designing and implementing efficient lighting solutions tailored to healthcare environments, ensuring optimal visibility, energy savings, and aesthetic integration. This project highlights ECDM’s technical excellence and its commitment to creating safe, functional, and welcoming spaces that serve both medical staff and the community.

Integrated Systems

Haya Karima

As part of Egypt’s national development program, ECDM was proud to contribute to the “Decent Life” initiative, delivering comprehensive low current systems across multiple service complexes and youth centers in rural communities. The scope included advanced systems such as CCTV, public address, access control, and data networking all tailored to enhance safety, connectivity, and operational efficiency. This project reflects ECDM’s commitment to community upliftment and technical excellence, ensuring that vital public facilities are equipped with smart, reliable infrastructure that supports Egypt’s vision for sustainable development.

Power Distribution

Haya Karima

Executed installation and commissioning of low voltage panels for the “Haya Karima” national initiative, enhancing rural infrastructure.

  • Executed specialized installation of low voltage electrical panels for youth centers, covering:
  • Detailed load analysis and distribution board assembly.
  • Selection and integration of circuit breakers, relays, and protective devices.
  • Integration of monitoring and control systems.
  • Factory Acceptance Test (FAT) and Site Acceptance Test (SAT).
  • Handover with on-site technical training for operational staff.
Access Control

Roche

Successfully executed the installation and configuration of Access Control Systems for Roch Company, in collaboration with Acciona. Scope included deploying secure entry solutions, system integration, and operational testing to meet international safety and performance standards.

Iconic Tower

Scope of work included smart integration setups, device configuration, and technical coordination to support centralized building management and automation functions.
