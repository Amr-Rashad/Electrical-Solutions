about Us

WHO WE ARE

ECDM Electric is a specialized solution provider in smart building technologies, offering advanced expertise in "KNX" automation, Building Management Systems "BMS", low current systems, and low voltage infrastructure. We design and implement scalable platforms that unify building operations transforming facilities into intelligent, responsive environments. Our strength lies in system integration. We connect technologies across energy, safety, and control domains to deliver centralized platforms that adapt, diagnose, and optimize in real time.

As part of our commitment to excellence, we collaborate with industry leaders such as SC Egypt, a trusted partner with a proven track record in infrastructure and operational resilience. This strategic alliance enhances our ability to deliver robust, future ready solutions across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Smart Integration Hub

Energy Management

Our Smart Integration Hub delivers a unified platform for advanced energy control. Through centralized programming and adaptive system design, we enable facilities to monitor consumption,optimize performance, and respond to operational issues in real time.

Built for resilience and efficiency, the system empowers smarter decisions and sustainable energy use.
