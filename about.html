About Us – ECDM Electric

We engineer integration. We activate intelligence. We build smart infrastructure.

about Us

ECDM Electric is a specialized solution provider in smart building technologies, offering advanced expertise in KNX automation, Building Management Systems (BMS), low current systems, and low voltage infrastructure.

We design and implement scalable platforms that unify building operations—transforming facilities into intelligent, responsive environments.

Our strength lies in system integration. We connect technologies across energy, safety, and control domains to deliver centralized platforms that adapt, diagnose, and optimize in real time.

As part of our commitment to excellence, we collaborate with industry leaders such as SC Egypt, a trusted partner with a proven track record in infrastructure and operational resilience. This strategic alliance enhances our ability to deliver robust, future-ready solutions across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors.

Our Mission

To deliver integrated smart solutions that connect technologies, enhance operational efficiency, and empower buildings to think, respond, and perform intelligently.

Our Vision

To lead the digital transformation of infrastructure—where every system speaks the same language, and every building becomes a dynamic, self-optimizing ecosystem.
